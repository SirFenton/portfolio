03 : About me

Background

I have over 12 years of experience as a financial accountant during which I have worked on all aspects of accounting and financial management. My higher education consists of BCom degree in Financial Accounting (North-West University) and Honors Degree in Financial Accounting (North-West University). Completed my SAIPA Articles in 2011 and from there I've been working in the IT industry, mostly for a JSE-Listed company.

History

I've always been interested in anything to do with technology so from an early age I've been tinkering with computers and gadgets. After entering the corporate world and working some years as a management accountant, I decide to learn a new skill or two. Worked some time on SAP and created a extensive budget and forecasting tool which made planning and consolidation a breeze. I then completed Microsoft SQL Database Developer courses and applied it to my daily task in the accounting environment and noted an area where the two worlds of data and finance work perfectly together. Since then I've completed a few Python courses which lead me to applying this language to web applications, data analysis, databases.
Everyday there is something new, like Albert Szent-Gyorgyi once said "Discovery consists of looking at the same thing as everyone else and thinking something different."

Passions

As you can gather, I'm passionate about IT and Finance and everything where these two world coexist. I also have a passion for adventure, new places, new experiences. To always enjoy every adventure one has to be able to adapt to any environment, be a quick learner and excellent and problem solving and critical thinking (luckily all skills a have honed)
